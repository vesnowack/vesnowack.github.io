Vesna Nowack

About Me

I am a Senior Research Associate at Lancaster University. I received my Ph.D. degree in computer architecture from Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya, Spain, in 2016. I became a teaching assistant at Technische Universität Dresden, Germany, in 2017, and a postdoctoral researcher at Queen Mary University of London, UK, in 2019.

Research Interest

My current research focuses on automatic program repair, in particular genetic improvement, generation of fix patterns, and application of repair tools in industry.
